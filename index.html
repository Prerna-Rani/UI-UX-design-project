What will you learn the workshop?

This workshop is divided into 2 parts, for you to learn and apply at the same time

Part 1: Essentials of Figma

  • Explore Figma's interface, workspace, and tools to kickstart your design journey.

  • Master text layers, shapes, icons, and color styles to build visually captivating designs.

  • Understand design principles like alignment, spacing, and hierarchy to create clean, structured layouts

  • Bring your designs to life with Figma's prototyping features, creating interactive and testable mockups.

Part 2: Hands-On Project

  • Apply your skills to design a fully functional landing page, perfect for a mobile or web app.

  • Gain practical experience by designing user-friendly layouts and applying responsive design techniques.

  • Showcase your polished project with prototypes and interactive elements ready for your portfolio.
